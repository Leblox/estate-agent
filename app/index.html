About us

When we started our family business back in 1986, we wanted to offer something different. We had three aims in mind: to deliver exceptional results on behalf of our clients, be honest and trustworthy and to provide the best customer service we possibly could.

Excellent service

Our independence and family ownership allow us to offer a level of service that others cannot match. We simply care more and try harder.

Local knowledge

With three generations of the family involved in the business, we are a family run company believing in traditional values. We know our local areas and have built relationships in the neighbourhoods over time.

No delays

Whether you are selling or purchasing, sit back and relax knowing that your transaction is personally handled by your agent at each step to avoid any costly or frustrating delays.
